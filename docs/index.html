💃🏻 Introduction

👩🏻‍🚀 Originally designed for the U.S. Department of Defense, Ada was designed with three overriding concerns:
  • Program reliability and maintenance, such as in The Military.
  • Programming as a human activity, such as in Engineering multi-team cross-field projects.
  • **Efficiency**, such as in Aerospace Control Systems.

    • 👩🏻‍🚒 Ada has gained a reputation as being the language of choice when software _**needs**_ to be correct.
